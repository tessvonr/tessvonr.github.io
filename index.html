Bioastronautics and Life Support Systems

Michigan's Bioastronautics and Life Support System (BLiSS) team is a student-run research team designing, building, and testing deep-space habitat prototype technology. The team conducts research in the Space Research Building on the North Campus of the University of Michigan. Founded in 2015 by Dan Abramov, the BLiSS team is comprised of both undergraduate and graduate students from multiple disciplines at the university.
