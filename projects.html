The Legend of Zelda (Unity Remaster)

The first dungeon from the NES game with an optional twist! Created by Ava Gizoni and Tess von Rueden.

My Contributions

I created some of the enemies you see in game: the Stalfos, Keese, Wallmasters, and Gels. I also worked on the player's health and UI. I made the game authentic with locking doors, item drops, the 2D bow room, and bombs. In the custom level, I designed the first 2 tutorial rooms along with the box sprites, UI, throwing mechanic, and the 4th room.

Technology Utilized

I worked in Unity and C# for this project.

How to Play

Use Z for secondary weapons and X for your sword. Press space to switch between your secondary weapons. Press 1 to enable cheats and press 4 to start the custom level.
