Bioastronautics and Life Support Systems

Michigan's Bioastronautics and Life Support System (BLiSS) team is a student-run research team designing, building, and testing deep-space habitat prototype technology. The team conducts research in the Space Research Building on the North Campus of the University of Michigan.
