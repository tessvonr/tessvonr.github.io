Sensorimotor Assessment Tool (SMAT)

During transitions from microgravity to heavier bodies (eg. Earth, Moon, and Mars) astronauts need to adapt or severe disorientation and motion sickness will occur. The SMAT project seeks to develop an obstructive sensor that monitor's astronaut's head and body motion in microgravity and develop metrics that can characterize astronauts' on-orbit head and motion.

ICON

NASA has contracted out ICON, a 3D home printing company, with the purpose of using their printers to 3D print habitats on the Moon using Moon regolith. The ICON project team is working with ICON to develop a simulation that will help design and analyze the mission infrastructure needed to print habitats on the Moon. The parametric simulation takes in tabulated data about a lunar region, a set of robots, and local infrastructure, and returns a simulated build schedule.

Cargo Assistant: Robotic Geometric Organization (CARGO)

Storing and retrieving provisions and equipment in a space-constrained area like the ISS is a challenge yet to be optimized. The CARGO team is developing an autonomous system for the Lunar Gateway Logistics Module to store and retrieve cargo for station crew while requiring less active crew time than the currently used methods on the ISS.

Multidisciplinary team of both undergraduate and graduate students.

Due to the breadth and diversity of topics on our projects students of all majors and school years are welcome, and encouraged, to join. Teams are comprised of freshmen to masters students who are still undecided, studying computer science, space engineering, kinesiology, and much more.

Our vision is to establish a Michigan student based team to lead the world in human spaceflight research.

We strive to lead projects focused on, but not limited to, the fields of bioastronautics and space life support systems and to advocate for an educational and purposeful approach to further enlighten the students and faculty about the field.

Fostering a sense of community is a significant focus of ours.

We are all a team and we strive to build our community through seminars, social events, and our meetings. We help each other improve and grow so we all can become better together and each contibute unique value to our projects.
